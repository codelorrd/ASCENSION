ASCENSION CREW FOUNDATION

Ascension crew foundation is a non-governmental organization set up with the sole purpose of contributing to positive change in the economic and social status of Africans, the less privileged, the vulnerable and minorities through effective programs that delivers sustainable outcome.

THE ORGANIZATION.

ACF is a regional non-governmental organization established in August 2022 and also registered with the Cooperate Affairs Commission, Abuja Nigeria (Kwara).

Motto:We Rise By Lifting Others.

More About Us

MISSION

MISSION

Building an equitable Society through constructive engagement with stakeholders, expand opportunities for self-development and realization among the youths, poor, minorities and Vulnerable Population.

VISION

VISION

An equitable Society where the voice of the youth, less privileged, poor, Vulnerable, minorities and communities is heard and their interest and welfare prioritized.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVE

  • To bring hope and aspiration to underprivileged students within Kwara state.
  • To increase the awareness of privileged students of the need to participate in community service initiatives .
  • To raise a great and substantial generation of change agents and notion builders.
  • To provide valuable and life changing materials to underprivileged students and children.
  • To improve educational outcomes of underprivileged students by enhancing teacher quality through appropriate professional development programmes.
  • To provide rock-bottom finance for he underprivileged within Kwara State region.
  • To create awareness for skill acquisition for the underprivileged a medium of eradication poverty within the region.

CORE VALUES

  1. Integrity and Transparency
  2. Mutual Respect and Discipline
  3. Fairness and Justice
  4. Humanity and Impact
  5. Professionalism and Team Work
